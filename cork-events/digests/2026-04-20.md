## ⭐ High-value opportunities

### ISPE Ireland INSPIRE 2026
- **When:** 14 May 2026, full day
- **Where:** Páirc Uí Chaoimh, Cork
- **Host:** International Society for Pharmaceutical Engineering (ISPE) Ireland Affiliate
- **Why it matters:** 600+ attendees from across Ireland's pharmaceutical and life-sciences sector. Strong director-level presence, a 60+ vendor exhibition, and a careers fair with the National Institute for Bioprocessing Research and Training (NIBRT). Free to attend — one of the single best Cork rooms for senior pharma/biotech networking this year.
- **Register:** [irishtechnews.ie — INSPIRE announcement](https://irishtechnews.ie/pharma-and-life-science-professionals-to-meet-at-ispe-irelands-inspire-conference-in-cork/) (Eventbrite: search "ISPE IRE INSPIRE 2026")

### TechFest 2026: Artificial Intelligence (AI), Technology & The Future
- **When:** 21 May 2026, 09:00–16:00
- **Where:** Rochestown Park Hotel, Douglas, Cork
- **Host:** Tech Industry Alliance (the merged it@cork + KerrySciTech, representing ~250 South-West tech employers)
- **Why it matters:** Described as the South-West of Ireland's leading technology conference. Keynotes, panels, and networking focused on how AI is reshaping business, governance and ethics. Attendee mix skews heavily to senior tech leaders, policymakers and industry experts — high-density director-level room.
- **Register:** [Eventbrite listing](https://www.eventbrite.ie/e/techfest-2026-ai-technology-the-future-tickets-1983943936721)

### Microsoft AI Copilot Event (Cork Chamber)
- **When:** 7 May 2026
- **Where:** Cork Chamber, 4 South Mall, Cork
- **Host:** Cork Chamber, in partnership with Microsoft
- **Why it matters:** A four-hour hands-on session led by Microsoft's Copilot and Modern Work experts, covering Copilot in Outlook, Word, Excel and Teams. Practical AI skill uplift plus exposure to Cork Chamber's member base (senior operators and C-suite from Cork employers).
- **Register:** [Cork Chamber — Microsoft AI Copilot Event](https://chamber.corkchamber.ie/events/details/microsoft-ai-copilot-event-36901)

### International Federation for Information Processing (IFIP) WG 8.3 Open Conference 2026
- **When:** 8–10 June 2026
- **Where:** UCC Business School, University College Cork
- **Host:** UCC Business School / IFIP Working Group 8.3 (Decision Support Systems)
- **Why it matters:** Three-day international academic conference on decision making and decision support — methods, analytics, stakeholders, business impact. Heavy research content for someone wanting science depth, plus faculty and senior researcher contacts at UCC. Strong multi-topic fit (operations research, data science, business analytics).
- **Register:** [UCC — IFIP WG 8.3 Open Conference 2026 Call for Papers](https://www.ucc.ie/en/business-school/newsandevents/2025/call-for-papers-ifip-wg-83-open-conference-2026-june-8th10th-2026.html)
