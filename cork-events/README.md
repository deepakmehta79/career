### UCC Mathematical Sciences Colloquium: Prof Sir Michael Berry, Fellow of the Royal Society (FRS)
- **When:** 1 May 2026, 15:00
- **Where:** Aula Maxima, University College Cork
- **Host:** UCC School of Mathematical Sciences — "Waves and Partial Differential Equations (PDEs)" Colloquium
- **Why it matters:** Sir Michael Berry is one of the most cited mathematical physicists alive — he discovered the Berry phase and works in the borderlands between classical and quantum theory. As a UCC alum this is almost certainly free to attend and gets you back onto campus alongside current UCC researchers and faculty. Rare calibre of speaker for Cork.
- **Register / info:** [UCC — Waves and PDEs Colloquium](https://www.ucc.ie/en/matsci/news/ucc-soms-waves-and-pdes-colloquium.html)

- **Where:** Páirc Uí Chaoimh, Cork

Free to attend — one of the single best Cork rooms for senior pharma/biotech networking this year.
